Great question.

DuoPoet is a combination art/programming excercise, where users can randomly generate "DuoPoems" from 2-5 "fragments", which are just random prompts from Duolingo.

While going through Duolingo's Spanish course, I found that due to the somewhat basic sentence structure, the ordering of the prompts sometimes created surprisingly dark or funny little short poems, so I wrote a web app to generate and store them.
